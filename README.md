# easyeffects-presets
## Some of the best online available presets curated for easyeffects or any other .json preset importing app

## These are slightly tweaked versions of online available presets.


### Music 1 uses Bass enhancer and crystallizer to enhance bass and high frequencies, , with the Maximizer being used both as a compressor and as a maximizer.

### Music 3 is specifically designed for use with sources that do not have volume issues, such as the output of certain music players which normalize their output, like VLC, SM Player & Audacious, for example. It adds "depth" and "sparkle" for that "live concert" kind of sound.

### Music 4 is a music preset for non-normalizing players and/or more dynamic music content that is more in need of level/volume correction.


Most of the presets are originally from [BayouGuru67](https://github.com/BayouGuru67/Linux_Audio)
